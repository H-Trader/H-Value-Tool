Welcome to the H-Value Tool

This is a powerful stock valuation tool designed to help you determine whether a stock is overvalued or undervalued based on Graham's and a conservative revised version. Enter a stock's ticker symbol, margin of safety, and growth rate to get started.

How Does This Tool Work?

The H-Value Tool provides two valuation methods:

  • Graham's Valuation: Estimates the intrinsic value of a stock based on earnings and margin of safety.
  • Revised Valuation: A more conservative approach to determine a stock’s price range. Note that it’s not more accurate, but rather more cautious.

Important: In the Revised Version, the Margin of Safety and P/E No Growth should be entered as percentages. These values may appear as decimals, but don’t worry—the tool handles them correctly. This will be updated soon for clarity.
