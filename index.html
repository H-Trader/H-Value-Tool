Welcome to the H-Value Tool

This is a powerful stock valuation tool designed to help you determine whether a stock is overvalued or undervalued based on Graham's and a conservative revised version. Enter a stock's ticker symbol, margin of safety, and growth rate to get started.

Access Valuation Tool Access Portfolio Manager

How It Works:

  • Graham's Valuation: Estimates the stock's intrinsic value using earnings and margin of safety.
  • Revised Valuation: A more cautious estimate, providing a conservative range for the stock's value.
  • Portfolio Manager: Create your own portfolio by entering a stock’s ticker symbol, the price you bought the stock at, and the amount you invested. Keep track of your investments in one place.
  • Stats: Gain access to valuable metrics like EPS, P/E ratio, analyst ratings, revenue, and other key business statistics that help you make informed investment decisions.

Note: In Graham's Valuation, the margin of safety is displayed as a decimal instead of a percentage. Similarly, the P/E No Growth in the Revised Valuation appears as a decimal but is correctly handled by the tool. This will be updated soon for clarity.
