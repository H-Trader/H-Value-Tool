How It Works

The H-Value Tool provides two key stock valuation methods:

  • Graham's Valuation: Estimates the stock's intrinsic value using earnings and margin of safety.
  • Revised Valuation: A more cautious estimate, offering a conservative range for the stock’s value.
  • Other Metrics: At the bottom of the page, you’ll find an overview of the business, including key statistics such as EPS, P/E ratio, analyst ratings, and more.
