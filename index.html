What Can You Do with This Tool?

The H-Value Tool allows you to perform a detailed stock valuation based on the principles of Benjamin Graham and other valuation methods. By entering a stock's ticker symbol, margin of safety, and growth rate, you can quickly get insights into whether a stock is overvalued or undervalued.

  • Graham's Valuation: Calculates intrinsic value based on earnings and margin of safety.
  • Revised Valuation: A refined approach to estimate a more accurate price range.
  • Key Metrics: View essential data such as P/E ratios, EPS, and acceptable buy prices.

Important: For the Margin of Safety under Graham's valuation and the P/E No Growth under the revised version, please enter these values as percentages. Don’t worry if they appear as decimals—the tool will handle them correctly.
